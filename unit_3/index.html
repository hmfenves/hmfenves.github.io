Hello! My name is Hannah. Here is a little bit about me and what I do.

I am a graphic designer and illustrator based in Austin, Texas. I specialize in content layout, branding, and typography. All of my work starts with a pencil and sketchbook which ensures my full understanding of interacting elements to create an intentional and personal finished piece. My design and illustration style differ slightly as I enjoy creating literal and clean layouts, while adding illustrated components when applicable.

Outside my studio, I can be found exploring Austin's plethora of coffee shops, hanging out at the lake, and teaching my Australian Cattle Dog, Scout, how to swim.
